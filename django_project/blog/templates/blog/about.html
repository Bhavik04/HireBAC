About HireBAC


Since 2021

HireBAC is an initiative that envisions to bring along people that can be mutually benefitted from each other. It is an online website where people can hire others to do a certain job or can even find work for themselves according to their skills.

This will also help people to reduce the efforts required in such things with much more variety of options to choose from.

Our Objective


• The chief objective of HireBAC is to provide jobs so that people can do something productive in their spare time.

• It focuses on providing work to those who do not posses much skills but still want to earn something.

• It also aims to help people who can’t afford expensive professionals to help them.

• This will also help people to reduce the efforts required in such things with much more variety of options to choose from.
